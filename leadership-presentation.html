The section

Math kids carry beyond the classroom.

Future Math is the section dedicated to the durable thinking skills kids actually use in the real world — spotting misleading information, reasoning through choices when answers aren't clean, finding strategy in the moves they make, and understanding the world around them.

🎯Judgment 🧐Reasoning 🧭Strategic thinking 🔎Sense-making 💭Abstract thinking
Why this matters

Parents themselves are asking for this.

The evidence

  • 👨‍👩‍👧Parent voice — surveys (Pew, Common Sense Media) show "thinking for themselves" and "problem-solving" outrank content recall in what parents want from schools.
  • 📊Skills reports agree — WEF Future of Jobs and OECD 21st-Century Skills rank analytical thinking, critical thinking, and problem-solving as the top durable skills.
"

In my own conversations with parents — what they care about isn't another A+. It's whether their child can think clearly, see through nonsense, and make smart decisions when nobody hands them the answer.
