Concept Note

Going Viral

Two videos, same views right now. One's about to explode. Can you tell which one — just from the data?
Mode: Wonders  ·  Band 2 (Age 10-12)  ·  8 screens  ·  ~6 min
Skill
Pattern Recognition
Math
Growth, Doubling, Prediction
Real-world
Media Literacy
Duration
~6 min
Why this matters
Can you tell if something is about to blow up — just by reading the early data?
AI gives you numbers. It can't tell you if 220 views is about to become 10,000 or stay at 250. Reading the shape of data = a skill this builds.

The Big Idea
Two things can look the same right now and have completely different futures. The pattern is hiding in the early data — if you know how to read it.
